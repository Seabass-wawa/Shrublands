Triple T Arrives In Shrublands

Wednesday, May 20th 2026

The Blue-Line in the Shrublands Railway System (SRS) got a new station: Triple T Station (Blue-Line 6)! This station connects the main shrublands area to the outer area specifically the Tung Tung Tung Sahur Statue located on the southern areas of The Shrublands. This Station makes it easy for users to go to the Statue without having to cross a body of water. This station also marks the new end of the Blue-Line (BL) Service.

This station is also the first station in the Shrublands Railway System (SRS) to have a staircase that directly connects the surface to the underground station!
