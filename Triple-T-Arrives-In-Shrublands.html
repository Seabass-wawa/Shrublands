Triple T Arrives In Shrublands

Wednesday, May 20th 2026

In the southern parts of the Shrublands claim, a Tung Tung Tung Sahur Statue suddenly appeared out of nowhere. It is later discovered that a player by the name Fluffgirl had built it when she was passing by the place. Alongside the statue, a giant 67 structure was also placed beside it.

Wialte, a member of the Shrublands community, discovered it first and posted it on the Betacraft discord server on May 20th, 2026 captioned "who made ts 😭". Although the builder wasn't discovered until kokobst found signs under the statue that says "made by fluffgirl".
