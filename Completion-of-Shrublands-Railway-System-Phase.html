Completion of Shrublands Railway System Phase I

Tuesday, June 2nd 2026

On June 2nd, 2026, a remarkable achievement has been attained by the Shrublands community: The Completion of Shrublands Railway System Phase I. The project took 36 days to finish starting from April 28th until June 2nd 2026. The Railways consists of underground sections and also above ground sections with stations that serves the entirety of shrublands. Over 30.000 Blocks were excavated for this project!

There were countless people that were involved in this project including builders such as J186 and rapciu. Overall, the whole project was planned by kokobst and wialte. They contributed the greatest in this project.

This completion finally connects the whole Shrublands all the way from end to end.

For the Railway Map, please refer to the Railway Page :D

Glory to Shrublands!
